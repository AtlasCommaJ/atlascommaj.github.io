Museum of Hangovers

A little of a play on words here. I have three months between the bar exam and when I start work. Post-bar, so "hangover," hah. A year ago in Zagreb, Peter and I visited the Museum of Hangovers one morning before we left for Slovenia. Remarkable place.

Not sure what I'll do with the time. I'll need to move, either straight to New York or somewhere else intervening. For the past day or two I've been kicking around the idea of finally getting into film. Not with the expectation of it turning into anything career-wise, and I suppose I'm doing less than everything I could be doing towards that if I did want it. But maybe as a hobby. My favorite part of this past year were the thirty minutes making the commercial for the Pub. Why not keep that going. Need to build some competency first.

I'll try to do something every day. I'll try to write every day about it, too. Something will come of it.
